maidr Live Multi-Layer Candlestick / Stock Ticker Demo

Click the chart (or Tab to it), then use arrow keys: Left/Right move between candles, Up/Down move between OHLC segments. Press PageUp/PageDown to switch between the price (candlestick), volume (bar), and moving-average (line) layers. Press M to toggle monitor mode: each new tick's close price, volume, and moving average are sonified and announced while you keep your position.
